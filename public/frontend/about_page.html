About HeartSync

A dating space built for calmer chemistry,
not louder chaos.

HeartSync started from a simple idea: most dating spaces push speed, pressure, and shallow swiping. This one is shaped around profile depth, softer pacing, and conversations that feel human.

Design Principles

What this website is trying to protect.

01

Less pressure

Profiles should tell a story before messaging starts, so every interaction begins with more context and less guessing.

02

More personality

Color, status, bio, interests, and presence all matter because people do not connect through one data point.

03

Frontend first

The right move here is to finish the client experience, then plug Firebase in once the screens and flow already make sense.

What Comes Next

Profiles to Firestore first, chats second.

That sequencing keeps the app simple. Save profile data to Firestore, load it into the profile and connection pages, then move the global room and direct threads from localStorage into Firebase collections.
